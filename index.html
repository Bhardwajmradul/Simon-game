Rules:-

  1. To start click any Key
  2. Now click the color flashed and remember color.
  3. Once you have click the right color, you would be promoted to the next level.
  4. Now will you will be flashed a sequence of colors and you have to remember it.
  5. Click the buttons in the same sequence as flashed
  6. If you clicked the colors in the right sequence then you would be promoted to the next level
  7. If you click the colors in the worng sequence then your game would be over and your final score will be flashed
